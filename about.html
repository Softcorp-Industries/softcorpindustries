Certified "Red Seal" Electrician Since 1996.

I started my Electrical apprenticeship in 1990.

For the first 8-10 years in the trade I worked as a Commercial Electrician. I moved on to the Oil Sands in the late 1990's as an Industrial Electrician. From 2000 to 2017 I worked mostly in the Oil Sands on huge projects for various Oil Sands Companies. I learned quite a few other skills in that time as well. I held positions in QA/QC departments on a few different Oil Sands Projects, this was where I learned a bit about databases and how useful they are (MS Access), I also learned more MS Excel skills. I've held Sub-foreman/Foreman/GeneralForeman positions in my career as well, I aquired management skills like Planning, Safety and Accountability. I've always loved building things growing up and into my Adult life, so along the way in my 30 years in construction I picked up quite a few other skills too, Plumbing basics, Carpentry basics, Drywalling & Taping, Painting and most general Handyman skills.
