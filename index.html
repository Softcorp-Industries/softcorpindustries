I Made my Own "Program"

Hello World!

The Industry I worked in for 20 years crashed a few years ago. The loss of jobs and unemployment was devistating to many families in my area. I lost my job too...

Going back to school at 50 was not an option, I didn't even know what to study.

I was completely lost, I had an idea that "Technology and the Internet" was the direction of the Future. What technology, and where on the Internet were two completely different questions.

By chance I came across a video on YouTube with this person giving a tutorial on HTML.

I started learning the basics of HTML and CSS with-in a few days of each other. I spent probably a month going a little more in-depth on both making little static pages, javaScript was the next logical step. I've been writing code for over a year now, I absolutely love it.

I still build things.

My tools just changed.
